TL;DR

After configuring fw1-loggrabber to collect logs from Check Point, here I analyze what all those fields mean. Depending on which product you are using (Check Point blades), log field names and format change.

Intro

What to do with all the information delivered by Check Point logs? What do they mean? The answer is to these questions until very recently was, I don’t know. Apparently these things are not documented anywhere reasonable until someone pointed out to me the LEA Fields documentation. Even my Diamond Support Engineer told me such documentation didn’t exist. He was wrong.

I understand that Check Point is a full featured product with many different functionalities. They do a good job in logging stuff but a lot of it does not make sense. In my case, I don’t do too many crazy things like enabling VPN, Endpoint or VSX. These are the following Check Point blades that I will cover here:

  • Firewall
  • IPS
  • Application Control/URL Filtering
  • DLP
  • Anti-bot/Anti-Virus

  • Identity Awareness
